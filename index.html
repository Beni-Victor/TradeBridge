Who We Are

We are more than a service provider. TradeBridge is a digital platform created to transform how trade works in Rwanda. Our mission is to connect suppliers, sellers, customers and drivers in one trusted ecosystem that makes buying, selling and delivering goods easier than ever.

What We Do

TradeBridge simplifies commerce by allowing suppliers to list their products online while sellers and customers can easily find them. Drivers then transport these goods efficiently, creating a reliable supply chain supported by technology.
