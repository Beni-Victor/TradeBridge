Who We Are

We are more than a service provider. TradeBridge is a digital platform created to transform how trade works in Rwanda. Our mission is to connect farmers, suppliers, sellers and delivery drivers into one reliable ecosystem where goods move faster and businesses grow stronger.

What We Do

TradeBridge simplifies commerce. Suppliers list products, sellers and customers discover them, and drivers deliver goods efficiently across Rwanda. By combining technology and logistics, we create a smarter way for businesses and communities to trade.
